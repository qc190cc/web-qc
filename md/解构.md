
# 解构 (ES6）
 - 让等号两边保持相等的结构

 - 交换位置 
	
		let a=1,b=2;
		[a,b]=[b,a]
		console.log(a,b) //输出为2 1
 
 - **数组解构**let[a,b,c]=arr;
		

		let arr=[1,2,3]
		let [a,b,c]=arr;
		console.log(b) //输出为2
		
		let [,,a]=arr;
		console.log(a) //输出为3

 - **对象解构**	
 	
		let obj={name:"zhangsan"};
		let {name:a}=obj;
		console.log{a} //输出为 zhangshan
		
		let {name}={name:"lisi"}
		console.log(name)//运用对像属性和属性名相同的简写,输出为lisi
